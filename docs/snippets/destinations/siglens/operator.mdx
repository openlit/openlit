### 2. Instrument your application
**For Kubernetes deployments with zero-code instrumentation:**

```yaml
apiVersion: openlit.io/v1alpha1
kind: AutoInstrumentation
metadata:
  name: siglens-instrumentation
  namespace: default
spec:
  selector:
    matchLabels:
      instrument: "true"
  python:
    instrumentation:
      provider: "openlit"
      version: "latest"
  otlp:
    endpoint: "YOUR_SIGLENS_HTTP_ENDPOINT"
    timeout: 30
  resource:
    environment: "production"
    serviceName: "my-ai-service"
```

Replace:
1. `YOUR_SIGLENS_HTTP_ENDPOINT` with your SigLens OTLP endpoint from Step 1.
   * Example (Local): `http://localhost:4318/v1/traces`
   * Example (Remote): `http://siglens.company.com:4318/v1/traces`

Refer to the [OpenLIT Operator Documentation](/latest/operator/overview) for more advanced configurations and use cases.
